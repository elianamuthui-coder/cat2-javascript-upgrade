I interviewed Lisa Waithera, owner of Yamali Creations.

Yamali Creations creates African-themed clocks, decorative mirrors, and handcrafted artwork.

The target audience includes art enthusiasts, tourists, and people interested in African-inspired home decor.

Lisa preferred a color scheme of gold, black, and sunset orange.

The purpose of the website is to showcase artwork, display prices, provide contact information, and encourage visitors to view the gallery and make inquiries.
